Who we are?

CLEO, a web-based portal that is created to make in-game purchases easier for Malaysian Gaming Community for multiple game by using a single platform. CLEO also strives to make transactions easier, faster, more secure & overall a more rewarding experience for consumers.

Established in 2021, CLEO is aiming to :

  • Offer the fastest and most reliable service
  • Offer reasonable price while maintaining the highest of standards
  • Make a functional and easily engage interfaces
